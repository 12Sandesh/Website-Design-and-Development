Streaming Sites

Sites recommended for watching anime

Animesuge

AnimeSuge is a free streaming anime website that allows you to watch anime online in English subbed and dubbed. Easy access and no registration is required. Our content is updated daily with fast streaming servers and great features that help you easily track and watch your favorite anime.

Crunchyroll

Crunchyroll is a site for watching anime shows. You can browse the site to enjoy the drama or animation forfree. This anime site is a joint venture between Japan’s Aniplex and Sony Pictures Entertainment. It also provides fans with a store that offers exciting anime merchandise.

9anime

9anime is a website that allows you to watch the animation. It offers numerous categories, including action, comedy, drama, games, and more. It allows you to watch subbed and dubbed versions of any anime without registering or paying.

Listing Sites

Sites recommended for listing anime

MyAnimeList

MyAnimeList, often abbreviated as MAL, is an anime and manga social networking and social cataloging application website run by volunteers. The site provides its users with a list-like system to organize and score anime and manga.

Kitsu

Kitsu is a modern anime discovery platform that helps you track the anime you're watching, discover new anime and socialize with other fans.

Anime-planet

Anime-Planet is the very first online anime and manga recommendation database that allows its users to watch over 45,000 anime episodes. The site claims to be legal and industry-supported due to partnerships with the anime industry.
