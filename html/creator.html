Animators

A creative specialist focusing on producing animated visuals for marketing, education, or entertainment is an animator. Animators create the appearance of motion by employing a variety of methods and resources to keep motionless objects or pictures alive.

Mangakas

A manga artist, or mangaka, is a person who makes the Japanese comic books or graphic novels referred to as manga. Mangakas are talented artists and storytellers who use a blend of spoken word and graphics to bring characters and stories to life.
